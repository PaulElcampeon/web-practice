About

Schizophrenia is a debilitating illness that affects approximately 1 percent of the population and only a quarter of sufferers overcome the illness enough to become functional members of society.

I have started this website to better that statistic, having had the illness myself for over 10 years. I have decided to share what has helped me to put myself in that quarter of sufferers and hopefully help others out of the illness as well. I am not all the way perfect yet, but well on the way.

My Blog

7 October 2020

2009

This was the year I was first sectioned. Things had not been right with myself for a long time. I had A-Level exams to complete before going to University. I think it was in the middle of examination season that things started to go wrong. Very wrong. I was doing four subjects and was booked in for 19 exams. I was told I could pick and choose which ones I go for. I was under immense pressure and was extremely overwhelmed. When I was on the commute to college I would be seeing ordinary people but believe I could see something extra in them. Something evil and sinister. I became very paranoid every time I left the house. The delusions increased and took on a more religious themed narrative. I thought everyone was in on it. My behaviour became extremely erratic and unusual at home to the point where my parents became very concerned. I ended up getting sectioned for three months and spent the entirety of summer in hospital.

19 September 2020

Why I am writing a blog

The premise of my website is to help other people out of this illness. Being ill in this way has caused me so much pain and heartache over the years. It is probably one of the worst things a young person can experience. When I left hospital in 2009, I lost all my motivation and drive, I didn't have my bath everyday and I did not have the impetus to do anything. I have come a very long way since then. I have completed a University degree and now have a job in education. The job seems to be going well at the moment. About 3 years ago I found a few good habits that have helped me to make this possible. These habits are included on my website. I am writing this blog to tell the rest of my story which I hope can encourage and inspire others in the same situation. Be on the look out for future posts:)
